FreeLance Websites

  • Utilized:HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, VSCode
  • date:2023 - Present

My freelance portfolio features two distinct website projects. The first is a content website for a creator, designed to showcase their videos, post schedules, and interact with viewers. This site includes links to all their social media accounts, a Discord invite for fan engagement, a carousel of their top three videos, and a play button for their latest YouTube upload.

The second project is a portfolio website for a photography student. It highlights their skills with categorized photo galleries and includes separate pages for their resume and skills. The site emphasizes their photographic talent and provides a comprehensive overview of their capabilities.

Showcasing dynamic and interactive web solutions tailored to client needs, from content creator platforms to photography portfolios.

Both projects demonstrate my ability to create user-friendly, interactive websites tailored to client needs. These websites showcase essential web development skills, such as front-end design, user experience optimization, and dynamic content integration. Looking ahead, I aim to continue developing solutions that enhance client engagement and effectively showcase their talents.
